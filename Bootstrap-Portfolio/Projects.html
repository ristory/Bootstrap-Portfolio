Electric Vehicle Chargers Wireless Security for Medium Business

The charging station only focuses on releasing the new models. They try to offer more power from the hardware side so the customer can charge their EV in the shortest time. From the software side, they give more functions to customers for managing the chargers, such as load management or the site controller. People just keep developing their products for the customer demand. However, we did not see that they gave an update or patch about security problems. My project provide a optimized solution to medium Business so they can mitigate the rish with minize budget

Accomplishments:

  • Follow NIST SP 800-53 and PCI DSS compliance to setup the devices
  • Charging sites have a reliable and secure wireless system
  • Charging site can protect customer information and block any malicious activities
  • Credit Card Transaction needs to be encrypted
  • Wireless networks need to install a firewall and set up the notification
  • Charging sites need to have a reliable power source

Cybersecurity Management – STRATEGIC

Company A doesn’t have a structed security program as well as detailed policies and standards to cybersecurity program. The organization lack security training to all employees, and it may lead to serious data breach. The most important thing now that we need to build basic standards regarding to Acceptable Use, Mobile Devices, Passwords, and PII to protect our organization assets.

Accomplishments:

  • Create the Security Report which includes physical, logical vulnerabilities and threats
  • Create detailed policies and standards for Acceptable Use, Mobile Devices Standards, Password, PII Standards based on NIST
  • Address the IT department duties that belong in the Compliance, Risk, and Security Department.
  • Develop Business Continuity Plan for Natural Disaster
